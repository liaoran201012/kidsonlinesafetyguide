About Kids Online Safety Guide

Last updated: June 2026

Kids Online Safety Guide is an independent resource that helps parents and guardians make sense of the growing world of parental-control and family-safety tools. Raising kids in a connected world is hard, and the marketing around these products often makes it harder. Our aim is to cut through that with clear, practical reviews and comparisons.

How we review

For each product we look at the features that matter most to families: what it can monitor, how alerts and controls work, which devices and apps it supports, how transparent its pricing is, and how easy it is for a non-technical parent to set up. We summarize both the strengths and the trade-offs, and we always encourage readers to confirm current details on the official product site before buying.

How we're funded

We keep our content free by using affiliate links. If you subscribe to a product through one of our links, we may earn a commission at no additional cost to you. This never changes our editorial assessment. You can read the full details in our Affiliate Disclosure.
